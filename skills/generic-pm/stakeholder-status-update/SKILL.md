# Stakeholder Status Update

Use this skill to communicate product progress and decisions clearly to stakeholders. Optimize for decision-making, not activity narration.

## Workflow

1. Read the current plan, roadmap, PRD, execution plan, validation results, risks, blockers, and recent changes.
2. Identify the audience and purpose:
   - executive update
   - team status
   - decision summary
   - meeting notes
   - risk escalation
   - handoff
3. Summarize:
   - current status
   - what changed
   - decisions made
   - risks and blockers
   - next actions, owners, and dates if known
   - asks or decisions needed
4. Keep technical detail only when it affects scope, risk, timeline, quality, or launch readiness.

## Output Rules

- Write in the language and tone appropriate for the audience.
- Do not invent owners, dates, commitments, or status.
- Separate facts from asks and recommendations.
- Prefer a short structure: status, changes, risks, decisions needed, next actions.
- Explicitly mention documented product, quality, security, compliance, or operational risks when they affect scope or release confidence.
